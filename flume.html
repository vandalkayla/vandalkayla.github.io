THE FLUME SLIDE & MOUNT LIBERTY

The Flume Slide trail is about a 7.5 mile hike up a 3,100 foot mountian near Lincoln, NH. It is a path that is less traveled than others and is more recommended for experienced hikers. There is about a 2 mile stretch that is almost vertical and it is all rock face. The view is beautiful which makes the long hike so rewarding once you've reached the top. Mount Liberty is another 4,000 foot mountain standing at 4460 feet! It is right next to the Flume Slide. The view, in my opinion, is much more beautiful from the top this mountain compared to the Flume Slide, but to each their own.
